    Our Mission

    To harness AI and machine learning to decode complex biological structures, enabling groundbreaking discoveries in medicine and synthetic biology.

    Our Vision

    To revolutionize microscopic imaging and analysis, paving the way for advancements in infectious disease research and therapeutic development.

    The RethaNova AI Lab combines cutting-edge cryo-electron tomography (cryo-ET) with artificial intelligence and machine learning to detect bacterial flagellar motors. Our research helps scientists uncover microscopic structures with precision.
